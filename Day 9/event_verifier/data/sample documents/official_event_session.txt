Title: AI Agents in Healthcare

Description:
This session explored how AI agents are being deployed in the healthcare industry. Topics included LLM-based diagnostic assistants, intelligent chatbots for patients, and case studies on AI in radiology and pathology.

Speaker: Dr. Priya Sharma, Chief AI Officer at MediTech

Key Points:
- Use of LangChain for building intelligent agents
- Case study: Radiology diagnosis using Gemini Pro
- Ethical considerations in autonomous decision-making
- RAG-based retrieval for medical record analysis
